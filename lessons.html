Background

Originally from Hawaii, I am an active violist currently based in Irvine, CA. I graduated with a Bachelor of Music Performance from the Eastman School of Music in Rochester, NY and a Master of Music Performance from the University of Southern California Thornton School of Music in Los Angeles, CA. My primary teachers include Carol Rodland, Masumi Per Rostad, and Karen Dreyfus.

Performing

I have performed with various orchestras including the Rochester Philharmonic Orchestra and Hawaii Symphony Orchestra. I also spent summers performing at classical music festivals, including Aspen Music Festival & School, Bowdoin International Music Festival, and American Institute of Musical Studies. My performances have taken me around the world from Hawaii to New York, California, Maine, Colorado, Germany, and Austria.

Teaching

I have taught private lessons to violin and viola students of various ages and levels. I previously taught at 88 Keys Music Academy in Arcadia, CA and SOMA Music Academy in Diamond Bar, CA. I develop specific teaching philosophies and learning plans for individual students, adapting and discovering creative methods for students of diverse backgrounds. During the pandemic, I also assist and integrate students and parents to online learning via Zoom.
